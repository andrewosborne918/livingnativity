Thanks for coming tonight! We hope you enjoy your time with us. We encourage you to use this app to follow along. As you approach each station, press play to hear the audio for that station.

On the right you will see a sign that reads “Together.” In one way or another, it is likely that we all have experienced separation and loneliness. If anyone understands separation, its God. Tonight, we focus on the fact that Jesus came into this world to bring us closer to God. Because of Jesus, we live together in love forever.
